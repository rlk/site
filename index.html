I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Louisiana State University and a member of the faculty of the Center for Computation & Technology. I study real-time 3D computer graphics, interactive display technology, digital imaging, and image processing.

This site catalogs my work. It includes courses taught, research done, talks given, installations, demonstrations, and handy source code.

My curriculum vitae includes all of my published and co-authored papers.
