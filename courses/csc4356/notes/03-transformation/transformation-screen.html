Screen Transformation

One final transformation maps the 3D scene onto the pixels of the screen.

\[S=\begin{bmatrix} w/2 & 0 & 0 & w / 2 \\ 0 & h/2 & 0 & h / 2 \\ 0 & 0 & d / 2 & d / 2 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 1 \\ \end{bmatrix}\] For example: \[\begin{array}{lcr} w &=& 1920 \\ h &=& 1080 \\ h &=& 65536 \\ \end{array}\]
