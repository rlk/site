CSC 4263 / ART 4020 — Video Game Design

Video Game Design is a team-based, project-centric course that covers every aspect of video game design and development. This course is taught collaboratively as CS426 with Prof. Jason Leigh at the University of Illinois at Chicago via high-definition video teleconference.
